Prayaas India

Prayaas India is a Non-Governmental Organisation providing free and high quality education to underprivileged children living in slums and villages near BIT Sindri college campus. The whole management of the organization is handled by college students. Presently more than 500 children are getting free education. Apart from two-hours evening classes, meritorious of them are admitted in private schools for formal education.
